# Getting Started with DigiKraft.shop

Welcome! This guide will help you make the most of your DigiKraft.shop experience.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Click **Register** in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile

## Step 2: Browse Products

### Search & Filter
- Use the search bar for specific items
- Filter by category, price, and rating
- Save favorites to your wishlist

### Product Details
Each product page includes:
- Preview images and demos
- Detailed descriptions
- File formats and sizes
- License information
- Customer reviews

## Step 3: Make a Purchase

1. Add items to your cart
2. Review your order
3. Choose your license type
4. Complete secure checkout
5. Download immediately

## Step 4: Download Your Files

After purchase:
- Go to **My Account** → **Downloads**
- Click download for each product
- Files are available forever
- Re-download anytime

## Step 5: Use Your Products

### License Guidelines
- Read the license terms for each product
- Standard license: Personal & commercial use
- Extended license: Resale rights included

- Check our [FAQ](/help)
- Contact support
- Join our community
