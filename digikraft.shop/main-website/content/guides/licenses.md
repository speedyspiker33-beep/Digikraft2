# Understanding License Types

DigiKraft.shop offers two license types. Choose the right one for your needs.

## Standard License

### What You Can Do
✅ Use in personal projects  
✅ Use in commercial projects  
✅ Modify and customize  
✅ Create unlimited end products  
✅ Use for client work  

### What You Cannot Do
❌ Resell or redistribute source files  
❌ Use in products for resale  
❌ Share with others  
❌ Claim as your own work  

### Price
Standard license is included with base product price.

### Best For
- Client projects
- Personal websites
- Marketing materials
- Social media content

---

## Extended License

### Everything in Standard, Plus
✅ Resale rights for end products  
✅ Use in templates for sale  
✅ Use in SaaS products  
✅ Unlimited distribution  

### What You Still Cannot Do
❌ Resell original source files  
❌ Create competing products  

### Price
Typically 3-5x the standard license price.

### Best For
- Website templates for sale
- Theme marketplaces
- SaaS applications
- Products with multiple users
