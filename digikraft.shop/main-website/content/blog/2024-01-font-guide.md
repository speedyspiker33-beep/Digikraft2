# How to Choose the Perfect Font for Your Project

Typography can make or break your design. Here's how to choose wisely.

## Understanding Font Categories

### Serif Fonts
Traditional and trustworthy. Perfect for:
- Editorial content
- Luxury brands
- Professional documents

### Sans-Serif Fonts
Modern and clean. Ideal for:
- Tech companies
- Minimalist designs
- Digital interfaces

### Display Fonts
Bold and attention-grabbing. Use for:
- Headlines
- Logos
- Short text only

## Key Considerations

### 1. Readability First
Your font must be legible at all sizes, especially for body text.

### 2. Brand Alignment
Choose fonts that reflect your brand personality and values.

### 3. Pairing Fonts
Combine complementary fonts for visual hierarchy:
- Heading: Bold display or serif
- Body: Clean sans-serif
- Accent: Script or decorative (sparingly)

### 4. Technical Requirements
Consider:
- Web font loading speed
- License restrictions
- Cross-platform compatibility

## Pro Tips

1. **Limit your selection**: Use 2-3 fonts maximum
2. **Test at scale**: View fonts at actual usage sizes
3. **Check contrast**: Ensure readability on all backgrounds
4. **Consider accessibility**: Avoid overly decorative fonts for body text

## Popular Font Combinations

- **Classic**: Playfair Display + Source Sans Pro
- **Modern**: Montserrat + Open Sans
- **Editorial**: Merriweather + Lato
- **Tech**: Inter + Roboto Mono
